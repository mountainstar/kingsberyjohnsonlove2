Overview

Abby C. Boyd is a partner with the firm. Her practice focuses on estate and tax planning and estate and trust administration.

Abby graduated from the University of Baltimore School of Law (summa cum laude) and before joining the firm was a law clerk for the Honorable Edward D. Bronfin in Denver District Court.

Practice areas

  • Wills and powers of attorney
  • Special needs and supplemental planning
  • Probate administration
  • Trust modification and distribution
