Estate planning

We provide estate, wealth transfer, and tax planning services to individuals, couples and families. From the most basic to the most complex, we prepare individualized estate plans designed to maximize our clients' flexibility and intentions while minimizing estate, gift, and generation-skipping transfer taxes.

Services include

  • Wills and revocable trusts
  • Powers of attorney and healthcare directives
  • Asset transfer and beneficiary coordination
  • Updates for life and tax law changes
