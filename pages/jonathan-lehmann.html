Overview

Jonathan A. Lehmann is a partner with the firm. His practice focuses on estate and tax planning; probate, estate and trust administration; advising family and closely-held businesses; and counseling executives and entrepreneurs.

Jonathan received his undergraduate degree from Wisconsin in 2000 (magna cum laude) and his law degree from Florida State University School of Law in 2005 (cum laude). He is admitted to the Federal Court of Claims.

Practice areas

  • Estate and gift planning
  • Business formation and governance
  • Contracts and transactions
  • Trust administration
