Who we are

Kingsbery, Johnson & Love, LLP is a boutique law firm located in Lafayette, Colorado. Our practice is focused on estate, wealth transfer, and tax planning services, as well as estate and trust administration.

The firm was founded in 1982 and serves Boulder County, the Denver metro area, and surrounding communities throughout Colorado.

Our attorneys have represented clients in:

  • Estate and tax planning for high-net worth clients with complex assets.
  • Estate, probate and trust administration with complex tax issues.
  • Adversarial probate and trust proceedings among beneficiaries.
  • Entrepreneur and family business entity and succession planning.
  • Marital agreement negotiations and executive contract counseling.
